Our Sponsors


ng-Grid is the leading Javascript Data Grid in the market. We are a company built by developers for developers, and - true to our roots - we offer ag-Grid Free: an open-source project that delivers world-class grid performance. ag-Grid Enterprise is our commercially-licensed offering which has enjoyed widespread adoption and permits us to keep delivering on our mission. Our story is proof that necessity is the mother of invention. During his time working in London-based financial institutions, Niall Crosby - founder and CEO - struggled to find any data grid component that could deliver the performance required in tandem with a complete feature list. This struggle ultimately led Niall to pull out the keyboard one Christmas holiday period and starting ag-Grid as a side project. This was then released as open source and quickly developed a following.
